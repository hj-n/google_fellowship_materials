Materials for Google Ph.D. Fellowship Applications

Hyeon Jeon, Ph.D. student, Seoul National University

These materials are used for my Google Ph.D. Fellowship application in 2024. Having been selected as a recipient, I have received several requests to share my application materials. As I benefited from publicly shared resources during my application process, I am making my materials available here. Note that I have also included links to publicly accessible materials that informed my application. I hope these resources assist you in your own application.

Research Statement

I attached the PDF of my research statement and the LaTeX source code. The design of the research statement is based on the ones provided by Dr. Fumeng Yang (Link) and Dr. Zhu-Tian Chen (Link). All the LaTeX source code is written by me and is fine-tuned to fit my contents, thus needs some hand-tuning to align with your contents. I recently found that Dr. Yang has publicized her template, which seems to be more polished than mine. You can find it here.
